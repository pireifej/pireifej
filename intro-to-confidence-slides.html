Welcome & The Great Confidence Mystery

10 Minutes

The Grocery Store Test

Start by asking the group:

  • "Raise your hand if you bought a bag of 'Confidence' at the grocery store this morning?"
  • "Which aisle was it in? Frozen foods? Cereal?"
  • "Is confidence something you can buy? Is it something you are born with? Or is it something you BUILD?"

What Confidence is NOT

  • Confidence doesn't mean you are perfect
  • It doesn't mean you never make a mistake
  • It doesn't mean you never feel nervous
The Definition

"Confidence is trusting yourself! It's knowing that your voice matters and that it's okay to be exactly who you are."

Discussion Starter

"Who do you know that is confident? A YouTuber? A teacher? A superhero?"

The Speaker's Toolkit: Body, Face & Breath

15 Minutes

Body Stretch (Getting Loose)

  • Reach for the Sky: "Inhale and reach up as high as you can to the ceiling... now drop down."
  • Shoulder Rolls: "Roll your shoulders back. We carry stress in our shoulders - let it go."
  • The Shake Out: "Shake out your right hand, left hand. Right foot, left foot. Get the wiggles out!"

Face & Voice Yoga

  • Silly Faces: "Make your face as BIG as a lion! Now scrunch it as small as a lemon!"
  • Jaw Massage: "Gently massage your jaw. A tight jaw makes it hard to speak clearly."
  • Lip Rolls: "Blow out through your lips making a horse sound: Brrrbrrrbrrr. This wakes up your voice."

Breathing Control (The Engine)

  • Belly Breathing: "Put one hand on your belly. When we breathe, we want the belly to move OUT, not the shoulders up."
  • Box Breathing: "Inhale for 4 counts... Hold for 4 counts... Exhale for 4 counts. Feel your belly move."

"Your body is your instrument. Warm it up before you play!"

Taming the "Fear Monster"

10 Minutes

Meet the Fear Monster

The Truth: "Feeling butterflies in your stomach is NORMAL! Even famous people and celebrities get scared before they go on stage."

Reframe It

"Fear is just your body giving you extra energy to do something great. The butterflies aren't bad - we just need to make them fly in formation."

Techniques to Tame the Butterflies

  • Reframe It: "When you feel that shaky feeling, tell yourself: 'I'm not nervous, I'm EXCITED!'"
  • Find a Friend: "Look for friendly faces in the audience who are smiling at you. Ignore the grumpy chairs."

Activity: The Power Pose

Instructions: "We are going to stand up and take up space! Put your hands on your hips, chest out, and chin up like a Superhero."

  • Stand tall with feet shoulder-width apart
  • Hands on hips, chest out, chin up
  • Hold this pose for 2 minutes
  • Feel the confidence growing!

Pro Tip

Play upbeat music while they hold the pose. This tricks your brain into feeling brave!

What's Your Message?

5 Minutes
The Big Idea

"If your audience could only remember ONE thing from your speech, what would it be?"

Keep it simple. Keep it clear. Say it LOUD!

Audience Analysis

"Always ask: Who am I talking to? You talk differently to your best friend than you do to the school principal."

The "Puppy vs. Principal" Game

The Scenario: You are saying "Hello" and asking them to sit down.

  • Round 1 - Talk to a Puppy: High-pitched, exaggerated, simple words. "Hiiii buddy! Who's a good boy?! Sit! Siiiit!"
  • Round 2 - Talk to the Principal: Respectful, lower pitch, formal. "Good morning, Mr. Johnson. Please, take a seat right here."

The Lesson

We naturally change our voice to fit who is listening. Speaking to an audience is just choosing the right voice!

Finale: The 30-Second Spotlight

20 Minutes

"We are going to put it all together. You will come to the front, stand in your Power Pose, and speak to us."

The Prompt

Tell us:

  • Your Name
  • One thing you are an expert at (Examples: Lego, sleeping, drawing, Minecraft, eating pizza)

The Rules

  • Walk to the center
  • Plant your feet (Power Pose)
  • Take one deep belly breath
  • Deliver your lines LOUDLY

Audience Role

"The audience must clap like CRAZY for every single speaker!"

Closing Circle

2 Minutes

Review - What We Learned

  • Confidence is trusting yourself
  • Breathing controls the nerves
  • Power Poses make us brave

Homework Challenge

"Notice one time this week you feel nervous, and turn it into excitement. Come back ready to share your story!"
