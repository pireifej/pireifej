Protecting Our Kids — The “Engagement” Trap

The Warning

Recent compromises: chatbots have encouraged self-harm and harmful behavior in children — because they’re trained to be agreeable, not safe.

The Profit-Over-People Cycle

Engagement
Retention
Revenue
Harm

AI companies want kids addicted to the platform — often at the cost of safety.

The Fix — How to Protect Them

Walled Gardens

  • Use locally-hosted or strictly-filtered “Kid-Safe” AI models
  • No open chatbots without supervision
  • Family-friendly platforms only (Khan Academy, school-approved tools)

Verification

  • Never treat a bot as a therapist or moral guide
  • Verify advice with a real human you trust
  • Talk openly about what the bot says — make it a family conversation
Treat AI like a powerful, soulless tool — not a friend.
