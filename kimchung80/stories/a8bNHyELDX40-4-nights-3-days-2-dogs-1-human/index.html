Kimberly Chung

4 Nights, 3 Days, 2 Dogs, 1 Human

Another Day Not in Paradise

I like having my head pat. *yawn* I'm kind of tired. If I close my eyes maybe my Paw Paw and Grandpaw will be back.
I guess they have fancy things out here too. I think one of these is worth 50 of Mom's cars. And that's being nice.
While waiting for my Paw Paw and Grandpaw, I'm starting to see how things shake out around here. After everyone eats, we go on a walk. Am I in the Arctic? Why do I need to wear a shirt already? Is this really Morristown?
Dot took me to some new sniffing grounds. I think I remember this place. She said Mom used to live here.
Hmmmm frozen custard time. I hope I get some.
I love what they've done with the landscaping.
I may be the Prince of Bay Ridge but Morristown doesn't know who I am yet. It's time to leave my mark.
Okay okay. Maybe Dot does some things better than me. Practice makes perfect. Can I get more? Please...
Gross. My sister always goes with the leaves under her butt. I personally prefer concrete. It's more civilized. Well I hope she doesn't get poison ivy or a rash.
Rumor has it that there's a magical car that will take me back to New York. Auntie talked about it. But will I know how to get back to Brooklyn? Do I dare??
Why does Mom insist on night walks? Even Dot wants to know why Mom is taking our picture. Doesn't she know the night is dark and full of terrors?
I didn't dare and I got a nervous stomach instead. How shameful I got caught going on grass.
This might be the lamest happy hour ever. It's like window shopping. All we get to do is look.
Rapido Rapido rapido!
