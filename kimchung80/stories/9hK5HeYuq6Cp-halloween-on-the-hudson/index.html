Halloween on the Hudson

It was a busy day for me. I'm so tired. It's time to dream of my favorite couch at home. Hope to see you soon down state!
And some treats.
Hi from upstate New York! Mom and Dad started packing their bags this morning and I didn't want to be left out of the fun.
We started our afternoon in downtown Tarrytown at an antiques store. It smelled funny in there but I was good and didn't pee.
Mom and Dad left me to have fun but they look cold.
There are lots of quaint shops in this town.
Greek place selling calamari too. Who would have guessed? This gyro was twice the size of a normal one but I didn't get too much. Yes Grandma, I'm filing a complaint.
It looks pretty cool but it would be better if they had some squirrels carved.
This coffee store has a Labrador as its mascot. I think rat terriers are better so I'm stepping on his face. My coffee shop would be called Coffee Roatsters. Like Coffee Roasters. Get it? Rooooo. Well I thought it was funny.
And stores that sell ridiculous costumes. Clearly I'm not a caterpillar.
FitBark says I made my goals already. Did Mom and Dad make their 10,000 steps??? All that walking calls for a nap.
