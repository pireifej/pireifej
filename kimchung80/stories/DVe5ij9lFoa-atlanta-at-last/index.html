Atlanta At Last

Goodbye Turner Field

Alas, it was time to go. I wish we had more time. I'm glad Auntie decided to take me out. I will miss Turner Field but I can't wait to see more of Atlanta.
Hi! I'm Chipper!! Six years ago, I was pulled from my home in Atlanta. I've met lots of nice people in New York and New Jersey but Atlanta is where it started. Nothing beats Southern hospitality!
After years of empty promises, I finally returned in time to say goodbye to Turner Field.
Glad to share an amazing view with my best friend, Gingy. We won't be able to see the city skyline from the new location.
Before we left, I made everyone take off their extra clothes. It's the south. We don't need sweatshirts here.
We took the shuttle bus like the locals. The Red Sox fans made it rowdy. And it made my blood boil.
We found Bowser. But why does his jersey say Rico Suave? He needs to play harder. I couldn't hear him over the Red Sox fans.
Do we need a caption? Best Braves player ever.
Auntie paid for upper box seats. I felt so special. Thanks!!
Stadium food is the best. I made Auntie eat a hot dog. She said she was full so I said it was okay to drink water. The sweet water stand wasn't selling tea. What do you think they were selling?
Something for everyone. While Geoff said the city was so hard to see, the Gusskis wished the Coke cannons worked.
It's just as I remembered it. I was happy to show my friends how amazing it is.
