4 Nights, 3 Days, 2 Dogs, 1 Human

Under the Oppressive Paw of Mom

Hmmmmm this leash makes it hard to escape...
Mmmmm bao. Reminds me of afternoons in Brooklyn. I don't know where everyone is but I miss you...
I haven't seen my Paw Paw and Grandpaw in a long time. I was so excited to go with them in the car yesterday. We went to see Mom but when I hopped back into my bag to go home, they took me for a walk, and then they were gone.
Playing CSI with Dot. Who marked this tree before? And what did they eat? Smells good but I made it smell better.
I waited by the door but then I knew they weren't coming back....
Mom took me on a walk. The leaves were really pretty but I just wanted to know where my Paw Paw and Grandpaw were. My sister tried to show me the ropes but I think I got this walking thing down pat.
Mom made me walk so much. I don't even walk that much in one day. No wonder my sister is so skinny.
The next morning I decided to make my escape. When Mom asked if I wanted to go out, it was the perfect chance to look for my Paw Paw and Grandpaw.
Instead, I was tortured with another walk. I didn't know my life was so easy. Please come back Grandpaw, Paw Paw... I promise to be good.
I thwarted Mom's plan to tire me out. After our nap, I wanted to search for my Paw Paw and Grandpaw. My sister is so easily fooled with belly rubs.
