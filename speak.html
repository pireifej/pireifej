Program Overview

What Students Will Learn

This comprehensive 8-week program empowers young speakers to build confidence, master communication skills, and discover their authentic voice. Students will learn practical techniques to overcome stage fright, structure compelling presentations, and connect deeply with any audience.

8-Week Curriculum

Journey to Confident Speaking

1

Introduction to Confidence

Overcoming fear, understanding the audience, and defining your message.

  • Techniques to manage stage fright
  • Audience analysis fundamentals
2

Impromptu Speaking

Developing the ability to think quickly and speak coherently off-the-cuff.

  • Quick thinking strategies
  • Table Topics practice
3

Speech Structure & Organization

Mastering the art of writing compelling introductions, bodies, and conclusions.

  • The power of strong openings
  • Logical flow and transitions
4

Vocal Variety & Body Language

Using tone, pace, volume, and non-verbal cues to enhance impact.

  • Voice modulation techniques
  • Powerful gestures and posture
5

Storytelling & Personal Narratives

Crafting and delivering a personal story that connects deeply with the audience.

  • Story structure elements
  • Emotional connection techniques
6

Speech Evaluation & Feedback

Learning to give and receive constructive, confidence-building criticism.

  • Effective feedback frameworks
  • Growth mindset development
7

Preparing for the Big Day

Rehearsal and final polish of prepared presentations.

  • Professional rehearsal techniques
  • Last-minute refinements
8

Live Community Showcase 🎤

A public speaking session for parents and community members at a local forum.

Your Instructor

Paul Ireifej

Paul is an award-winning public speaker and communication coach with over a decade of experience in Toastmasters International. He has competed at the district level in speech competitions and has facilitated workshops for students, professionals, and community organizations across New Jersey.

Paul's passion for empowering young speakers stems from his belief that confidence in communication opens doors to limitless opportunities. His engaging teaching style combines practical exercises, real-world examples, and supportive feedback to help students discover their authentic voice.

"Every student has a unique voice worth sharing. My goal is to help them find it, own it, and use it to make a difference."
