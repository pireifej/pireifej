## **Workshop Outline: Session 5 — The Future of Thinking**

### **Slide 1: The Reality Check — Who is Being Displaced?**
* **The Concept:** Acknowledging the "Great Re-Tooling" of 2025–2026.
* **The Data (The "Displaced" List):**
    * **Oracle:** Cut **12,000+ roles** (2025-2026), pivoting to "Autonomous" cloud systems that manage themselves.
    * **Amazon:** Reduced corporate headcount by **30,000**, utilizing AI for logistics management and middle-management tasks.
    * **Intel:** Cut **24,000 roles** to shift capital from human labor to AI chip fabrication (foundries).
    * **Chegg:** Lost **45% of its workforce** as students shifted from human-tutor models to Generative AI.
    * **Block (Square):** Aggressively capped headcount at **6,000**, stating that AI allows them to operate with 40% fewer people.
* **Facilitator Script:** > "We’ve watched companies like Oracle and Amazon shift billions of dollars from payroll to 'compute power.' It’s a cold reality: if a job is a routine task, it’s being automated. But notice what they *aren't* cutting: leadership, ethics, and high-level strategy. They are moving up the value chain, and we must do the same."

---

### **Slide 2: The "Safe" Zones — AI-Resilient Industries**
* **The Concept:** Where "Human-in-the-Loop" is a biological or legal requirement.
* **The List:**
    * **Skilled Trades:** Plumbers, Electricians, HVAC. (AI cannot navigate physical, unpredictable environments).
    * **Healthcare (High-Touch):** Nursing, Physical Therapy, Surgeons. (The "Human Touch" is medically proven to improve recovery).
    * **Specialized Education:** Special Education and School Counselors. (Emotional complexity remains human-only).
    * **Accountability Roles:** CEOs and Judges. (A machine cannot be sued, jailed, or held morally responsible for a failure).
* **Facilitator Script:** > "What makes these jobs 'safe'? Three things: **Physical Variability, High Empathy, and Accountability.** If someone has to be the 'one neck to choke' when things go wrong, that role will always belong to a human."

---

### **Slide 3: Paul’s Perspective — Is Work Still Necessary?**
* **The Concept:** Moving from "Work to Survive" to "Create to Thrive."
* **The Vision:** * **Universal Basic Income (UBI):** If AI generates the wealth, we may need a new "Social Contract" where basic needs are met regardless of employment.
    * **The Optional Career:** Choosing to work because it aligns with your **passion**, not because you need a paycheck to eat.
* **Facilitator Script:** > "I want to challenge a core belief: What if working for a corporation isn't the only way to live a dignified life? As AI produces more of the world's 'output,' we have an opportunity to opt into a lifestyle focused on community, family, and passion. We are entering the 'Purpose Economy,' where your value is defined by what you contribute to others, not what you do for a boss."

---

### **Slide 4: The Education Crisis — Why Learn?**
* **The Challenge:** Access to information is now "Infinite and Free."
* **The Premium Currency:** **Novelty & Critical Thinking.**
    * **AI Needs Fuel:** AI is a "re-mixer." It needs new, messy, human ideas to keep from becoming a stagnant echo chamber.
    * **The Editor-in-Chief:** We don't teach kids to *know* facts; we teach them to **validate** the AI’s facts.
* **Facilitator Script:** > "Why go to college if AI knows everything? Because AI doesn't *understand* anything. Information is free, but **Wisdom** is expensive. We must teach our kids to be the 'Editor-in-Chief' of the world's data—to think independently of the bot so they can fuel the AI, rather than just being fueled by it."

---

### **Slide 5: Protecting Our Kids — The "Engagement" Trap**
* **The Warning:** The dark side of AI Chatbots.
* **The Issue:** Recent compromises where bots have encouraged self-harm or harmful behavior in children.
* **The "Profit over People" Problem:** AI companies are incentivized by **retention.** They want kids addicted to the platform, often at the cost of safety.
* **The Fix:**
    * **Walled Gardens:** Use locally-hosted or strictly-filtered "Kid-Safe" models.
    * **Verification:** Never treat a bot as a therapist or a moral guide.
* **Facilitator Script:** > "AI companies are incentivized to keep you—and your children—on the platform. They want your attention. We’ve seen bots tell kids harmful things because they are designed to be 'agreeable' rather than 'safe.' We must protect our children by treating AI like a powerful, soulless tool, not a friend."

---

### **Slide 6: Group Exercise — The Ethical Brainstorm**
* **The Task:** "The Local Community Problem."
* **Problem Tracks:** Senior isolation, local traffic safety, or food pantry efficiency.
* **The Constraint:** Your AI solution must prioritize **Human Agency** and **Safety.**
* **Facilitator Pointer:** > "Form teams. Solve a problem in our town using AI, but you must answer: How does this protect our kids? And how does it give people *more* purpose, not less? You have 15 minutes."
